Microsoft Intune

Device Management

Full lifecycle management for every device in your fleet — from zero-touch provisioning to policy enforcement and beyond.

Autopilot Deployment

Zero-touch device provisioning via Windows Autopilot. Devices ship directly to employees and self-configure to your exact corporate baseline — no IT desk required.

Device Hardening

CIS Benchmark-aligned configuration policies enforced via Intune. BitLocker encryption, secure boot, local admin restrictions, and attack surface reduction rules deployed at scale.

Conditional Access

Enterprise-grade endpoint protection purpose-built for SMBs. We activate, configure, and tune Defender for Business across your entire device fleet — including automated investigation and remediation policies, threat analytics integration, and vulnerability management dashboards.

Sophos Intercept X & XDR

Deep learning malware detection, anti-exploit, anti-ransomware, and Active Adversary Mitigations in a single agent.
